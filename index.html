Industry Experts & Alumni We've Talked To

BILL GUTTENTAG

Bill Guttentag is a double Oscar-winning dramatic and documentary film writer-producer-director. His films have premiered at Sundance, Cannes, Telluride, and Tribeca film festivals. He has also been teaching at Stanford Graduate School of Business since 2001.

STERLING K. BROWN

Sterling K. Brown ('98) is a three-time Emmy Award Winning actor. His credits include starring as Randall Pearson on the NBC drama 'This is Us' (2016-2022), and as Christopher Darden in 'The People v. O.J. Simpson: American Crime Story' (2016). Some of his other credits include 'Black Panther' (2018), 'Waves' (2019) and 'The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel' (2019).

JAY ROACH

Jay Roach ('80) is an American film director & producer. He is best known for directing the Austin Powers film series (1997-2002), Meet the Parents (2000), Dinner for Schmucks (2010), The Campaign (2012), Trumbo (2015), and Bombshell (2019).

AMY ANIOBI

Amy Aniobi ('06) is a Nigerian-American writer and producer. She is best known for being a head writer and co-executive producer of Insecure (2016-2021) and also as an executive producer for the HBO special 2 Dope Queens (2018-2019). She has her own production company, SuperSpecial, and is under a TV overall deal at HBO/Max.

Professional Expertise

Our community includes professionals who have worked at Netflix, Disney, HBOMax, Paramount, Universal Music Group, amongst other companies. Networking and mentorship are major aspects of our organization.
